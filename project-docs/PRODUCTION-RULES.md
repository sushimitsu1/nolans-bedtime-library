# Story and Artwork Production Rules

## Book format
- 1 cover + 15 story pages
- Landscape 4:3
- Approx. 5 minutes per story
- 1–3 short sentences per page
- One clear action or idea per page
- Calm bedtime ending

## Tone
- Friendly, gentle, playful, reassuring
- Small problem, teamwork, safe resolution
- No injuries, frightening danger, aggressive destruction, or intense crashes

## Visual style
- Bright, polished, friendly 3D children's animation
- Large expressive windshield eyes
- Simple friendly mouths
- Oversized vehicle features suitable for preschoolers
- Consistent character design across every page
- Warm, readable composition
- No photorealism
- No dark horror treatment

## Page layout
- Illustration fills the page
- Rounded cream text panel across the bottom
- Large dark rounded font
- White circular page-number badge with blue outline at bottom-right
- Keep text away from important faces and action

## File rules
- Save as optimized WebP
- Story folder:
  `assets/books/<story-id>/`
- Required files:
  - `cover.webp`
  - `page-01.webp` through `page-15.webp`
- No zero-byte files
- Keep filenames lowercase and stable
- Do not rename existing story IDs without updating all references

## Monster Jam-inspired books
The user selected “Option B”:
- Real truck names may appear in story text and titles
- Visuals should be only loosely inspired
- Avoid exact logos, sponsor decals, exact liveries, and exact replicas
- Keep artwork child-friendly and distinct from official merchandise
- Treat these as private-family-use stories in a publicly reachable app
